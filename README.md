# GreenProcure-AI
AI-powered platform that helps companies make sustainable procurement decisions in real time. It detects hidden emissions, recommends greener suppliers, and provides a strategic mix of Reduce, Offset, and Own actions to drive measurable climate impact.

## Problem
Companies lack real-time visibility into procurement emissions and rely on carbon credits after decisions are made.

## Solution
GreenProcure AI helps reduce emissions at the point of decision and builds a long-term climate strategy.
